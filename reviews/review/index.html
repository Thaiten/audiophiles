Build Quality

Comfort

The DT 770 is surely not on the light-side. 287 g without the cable. But they are comfortable anyway. The earpads are fairly large so that they fit snugly around the ear. Also, I use the velour pads which I like very much. Doesn't give you that cold feeling when you put them on, although it can get quite hot in the summer. The whole headband is covered with paddings also, distributing the weight on your head. Everything is switchable, so if you'd like to change your earpads to leather, that isn't a problem. I actually hat to change the earpads because they got dirty due to heavy usage.

Travelability

This is a Studio-Headphone, so obviously it isn't great for traveling or just carrying around. Also, there is a possibility of the plastic cracking where you twist the headphone. This happened to me on both sides. You can see it here:

Unfortunately, but overall it's not like I'm afraid the headphones fall apart every next moment.
